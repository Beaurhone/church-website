About Shammah Integrity Prayer Centre

Shammah Integrity Prayer Centre is a vibrant, Spirit-led ministry dedicated to raising a people of prayer, holiness, and divine impact. Founded on the unshakable Word of God and led by anointed ministers, we are passionate about seeing lives transformed through Jesus Christ.

Our Mission

To spread the love of Christ and build strong spiritual foundations through teaching, prayer, worship, and outreach.

Our Vision

To be a house of refuge and transformation where God's presence dwells, and where destinies are restored for Kingdom impact.
